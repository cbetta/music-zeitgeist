UK Top Single Charts

This Music Zeitgeist is my little attempt to experiment with alternative visualisation techniques to display musical culture shifts.

This project was made for the first Music Hack Day in London in 2009 and relies on the EveryHit charts, LyricWiki API, and the Wordle generator utility.
